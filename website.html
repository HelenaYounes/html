INTRODUCTION TO SHARKS
Ancient Sharks lived before land vertebrates populated earth and even before than many plants developed on continents. While they were very different from those found today, sharks have been on this planet for long time. The shark species as we know them have been around for 100 million years. This means that they were here when dinosaurs were around. A record breaking evolutionary creature cannot be anything but amazing, with an anatomy which has been improved over those million years of evolution.
